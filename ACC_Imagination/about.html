The Clarke Center is creating a new theoretical framework for exploring imagination. Insights from the sciences, humanities, arts, engineering, and medicine are transforming the way we understand the basis of human's most remarkable gift: imagination.

More specifically:

  • research at the neural, behavioral and social level into how imagination occurs;
  • develop more effective ways of using imagination in education and learning; and
  • outreach to enhance appreciation of
  • imagination in confronting humanity’s problems.
