Art in Science Education

Every year the Clarke Center organizes or co-organizes three to four public programs intended to stimulate imagination. We also host the Clarion Foundation annual science fiction writers’ workshop. Shown below are some of the events we hosted so far. More are in planning. Anyone with a powerful idea and a will to co-organize should contact us.
