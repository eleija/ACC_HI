Science on Dance

Dance and Choreography is an understudied activity in Cognitive Science. It is latent with scientifically interesting phenomena such as distributed creativity, distributed memory, gesture, joint activity, joint thinking, thinking with the body, theory of practice, multimodal coordination, and more.
